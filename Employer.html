Employer

- Aurora Cannabis

Aurora Cannabis is a Canadian licensed cannabis producer founded in 2013. The company is a leader in the cannabis industry, focusing on the production and sale of medical and recreational cannabis products. Aurora operates multiple facilities across Canada and globally, with a strong presence in Europe, Australia, and other international markets. The company is known for its high-quality cannabis strains and innovative products, including oils, edibles, and vaporizer pens. Aurora emphasizes sustainability and uses state-of-the-art technology in its cultivation processes. The company is also committed to corporate social responsibility and the well-being of its customers and communities.

Fascinating Tidbits

  • Aurora Cannabis operates one of the world's largest and most technologically advanced indoor growing facilities in Edmonton, Canada.
  • The company was one of the first to be listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange's (TSE) cannabis index in 2018.
  • Aurora has a vast network of partnerships, including collaborations with research institutions and healthcare providers, to advance cannabis-based treatments.
  • The company is committed to sustainability, using renewable energy sources and implementing water conservation strategies in its production processes.
  • Aurora has expanded globally, with operations spanning over 20 countries, including a growing presence in Germany and other European nations.
  • Aurora has pioneered the development of cannabis-based beverages, one of the most innovative trends in the industry.
